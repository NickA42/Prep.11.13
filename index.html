How did Dr.B create the transition?

For starters, based on chapter 9 of the reading, Dr.B created those transitions by using a .transition() link. This link has to be placed after the .data link and before the .attr() link. The link .transition is used to transform one graph to another, it is much simpler in d3 than one may think it is. D3 basically does all of the work for you, it takes the beginning and ending values of a subject and inserts those into their calculation of the duration of the transition. For the duration of the transition, Dr.B simply used a .duration() link. This link is used to provide the transition with a time set on how long it will take to make the transition. Within the parenthesis of the .duration() tag, you put in numbers but in milliseconds. So if you want a 1 second transition, you put (after the duration tag) 1000 because 1000 milliseconds is 1 second.
